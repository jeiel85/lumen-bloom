# Goal 09 — Content and Feel Polish

## Objective

Reach launch content quantity and tune the complete experience.

## Work

- Final eight stage presentations.
- Full cosmetic catalog.
- Achievements and difficulty modes.
- Stage modifiers.
- Original key art/icon/store screenshots.
- First-session tuning.
- Balance simulation reports.
- Audio mix pass.
- Haptics adapter for future native wrapper, disabled on unsupported web.
- Credits and asset provenance.

## Acceptance criteria

- Launch content count is met.
- First expansion/session targets are met in playtest.
- No content resembles the reference game's named items or presentation.
- Every asset has provenance and license.
- Calm and Abyss modes are meaningfully distinct.
